Switch outputs instantly

Every output device one click away — headphones, speakers, displays, AirPlay — with the active one always marked.

Bluetooth, handled

Paired headphones show up even when disconnected: one click connects them and routes audio there automatically. Disconnect from the same list.

A fader for every app

Tame a loud browser without touching your music. Volumes persist per app across launches.

Native and lightweight

Swift and Core Audio process taps. No kernel extensions, no virtual drivers, no background bloat.

Private by design

No telemetry, no analytics, no network access. Audio never leaves the playback pipeline on your Mac.
